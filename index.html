Our Courses

At our photography school, we offer a variety of courses that cover all aspects of this fascinating art.

Basics of photography

During this course, you will familiarize yourself with different types of cameras, and learn the basics of composition.

Landscape photography

In this course, you'll learn how to choose the right angles, frame landscapes, and create depth and perspective in your images.

Portrait photography

During this course, you will learn to choose the right lighting, and reproduce the mood and expressions on the faces of the models.
